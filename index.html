Who We Serve

State Organizations

This program focuses on populations that join SNAP and includes life skills, employment readiness, and financial literacy to help foster a successful path forward.

Veterans

Our Veteran program focuses on tools supporting mental health and developing life skills to enhance their lives beyond their time of service to our country.

Juvenile Facilities

This platform targets youth in juvenile detention and alternative school facilities to offer life skills, financial literacy, and employment readiness that leads to a productive adulthood.

Criminal Justice

Our criminal justice program works with individuals that have had convictions and are looking for assistance with housing, job placement, mental health services, opening a bank account along with a multitude of partners that offer discounts on everyday necessities and incentivized learning.

At Harvest Solutions, we believe in the power of sports and wellness to enhance both physical and mental well-being. Our Sports and Wellness section serves as a comprehensive guide to all the support and activities we offer, designed to help you achieve a balanced and healthy lifestyle.

Positive Living Events: Engage in events and workshops that encourage a positive and active lifestyle, fostering overall well-being.

Housing with Harvest

At Harvest Solutions, we recognize the importance of a safe and comfortable place to call home. That's why we collaborate with our trusted housing partners to offer a wide range of opportunities that help you find the perfect living situation. Our goal is to connect you with the best housing options available, ensuring that you have a place where you feel secure and at ease.

Variety of Housing Options: From affordable apartments to long-term rentals, explore diverse housing opportunities that suit your needs and budget.

Personalized Housing Support: Receive expert guidance throughout the entire housing process, from search to move-in, ensuring a smooth transition.

Incentives & Rewards Program

At Harvest Solutions, we believe in recognizing and rewarding our community members for their continued engagement and commitment to personal growth. Our Incentives & Rewards Program is designed to keep you motivated, educated, and connected while offering you the chance to enjoy exclusive benefits.

Earn Rewards Through Engagement: Stay active within the community by participating in events, completing educational modules, and more to earn points.

Redeem for Premium Brands: Use your accumulated points to redeem rewards from top lifestyle brands, offering you exclusive deals and discounts.

Exclusive Offers: Gain access to special offers and promotions available only to members of our Incentives & Rewards Program.
